SMS Consent for Internal Providers

BeWell CRM sends SMS notifications to internal providers (employees and contracted clinicians) for operational purposes, including notification of new client intake assignments and follow-up reminders.

Providers provide consent to receive SMS notifications during onboarding by supplying their mobile phone number and agreeing to receive business-related text messages as part of their role.

SMS messages are non-marketing and limited to operational notifications. Messages may include a secure, time-limited link to view intake details after authentication.

Providers may opt out at any time by replying STOP. For help, providers may reply HELP.

Questions may be directed to internal support.
